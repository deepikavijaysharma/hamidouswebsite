ASR/CRM Territory Lookup Tool

ACCOUNT & SALES REP LOOK UP TOOL [Read More...]

The ASR (ACCOUNT & SALES REP LOOK UP TOOL) Tool is designed for Sales/Channel Managers, Sales/Channel Reps, BDCs, Pre-Sales, Sales Operations and any other valid Fusion CRM user to allow them to search for account coverage in Fusion.. All of them have full search access across all regions. Apart of it, the tool provides additional functionality for Sales/Channel Rep and Sales/Channel Managers. Sales reps can also retrieve the accounts assigned to their territory. A Channel Rep can also retrieve the partners setup on their territory. Sales Managers can also retrieve all accounts assigned to the reps that roll up to them. Channel Manager can also retrieve all accounts set on the territories for the reps that roll up to them.

This tool is designed to answer the following questions:
• Which of the Reps is assigned (Sales Rep)/setup (Channel Rep) to a specific account?
• What other co-prime reps are assigned/setup to that account?
• How many accounts are assigned/setup to a particular Sales/Channel Rep?
• List the accounts assigned/setup to a Sales/Channel Rep, sorted by size of account
